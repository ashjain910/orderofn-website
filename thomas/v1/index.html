About Thomas Grozier & Son

Thomas Grozier & Son Pty Ltd is a fourth generation family business that has been producing quality lubricants for over 80 years with an unwavering commitment to excellence.

We have grown from a National Supplier of quality lubricants to one that supplies products around the world. This growth has been achieved through constant consultation with customers, suppliers, staff, and accreditation organizations that all share our ongoing commitment to quality.

Our goal is to continue partnerships with companies that supply clean drinking water to communities around the world.

"Our aim is to supply quality product on time, every time, to ensure complete customer satisfaction."
